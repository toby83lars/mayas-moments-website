About

Hi, I'm Maya.

Born and raised in the heart of Minnesota, I made the exciting move to sunny Southwest Florida in 2021 — and I've been inspired by the vibrant landscapes and coastal charm ever since.

My fascination with photography began at a young age. There's something truly magical about capturing a fleeting moment and preserving it forever. While I enjoy being in front of the camera sometimes too, there's an undeniable thrill behind the lens.

My artistic journey didn't start with photography. Throughout my school years, I explored various art forms, honing my creative skills and developing a keen eye for composition. That deep appreciation for art translates directly into the way I capture and edit every photograph.

I had the incredible opportunity to serve on my high school yearbook team, documenting all the special moments that make up the high school experience. It was incredibly fulfilling to help preserve those memories for my classmates — and honestly, that's where I truly caught the photography bug.

At Maya's Moments, it's not just about capturing moments; it's about transforming them into beautiful artworks. Each photo I take is a chance to create a lasting piece of art that tells a unique story.

Whether it's a joyful engagement, a dream wedding, a maternity session celebrating new beginnings, or a heartwarming family portrait — I'm here to document your journey with a creative touch.
